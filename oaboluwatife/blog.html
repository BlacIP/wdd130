The blog section of my site will document my ongoing journey through the world of web design and development. Expect to find posts covering:
1. Lessons learned from online courses, tutorials, and books on HTML, CSS, JavaScript and other coding languages
2. Reflections on UI/UX design best practices for website layout, navigation, forms, etc.
3. Reviews of design inspiration sources like Dribbble, Behance, and Awwwards.
4. Highlights from web development conferences and networking events.
5. Tips, tricks and resources for beginning designers and devs.

6. Things I tried, struggled with, and ultimately figured out.
7. Web design and development news, trends, and innovations.
8. Interviews with professionals across the industry.
9. Thoughts on building an effective personal brand and portfolio.
10. Strategies for transitioning into web design as a career.
I aim to provide value to those starting out like me or considering a career pivot. Please subscribe or check back often as I chart my course in the world of web design and development. I look forward to exchanging ideas and connecting with fellow creatives
