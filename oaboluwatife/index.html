Welcome
Thank you for visiting my personal website. My name is Boluwatife and I created this space to chronicle my journey as I explore the ever-evolving world of web design and development. On this home page, you'll find a portfolio of select web development and UI/UX design projects that I've worked on. Each project includes an overview, explanatory images and/or videos, and key takeaways from my experience. My goal is to give you an inside look at my skills, creative process, and growth over time.

Please feel free to look around! I welcome any feedback or thoughts on my work so far. As a lifelong learner who is passionate about creating intuitive digital experiences, I look forward to documenting the projects and progress I make on this journey. Ultimately, I aim to become an expert in designing and developing accessible, responsive websites that connect with users and bring value to businesses and organizations. Follow along if you're interested in a beginner's perspective on picking up these skills!
